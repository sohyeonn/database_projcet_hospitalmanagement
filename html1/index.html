Sejong Hospital

Sejong University Hospital is effectively managing patients through its database. Patients can view their previous records at a glance through previous patient records. You can also choose your own professor and choose a hospitalization room. In addition, the costs and peculiarities that can be known only through the staff can be found directly on the website..

Sejong University Hospital has 11 departments. Surgery, Internal Medicine, Thoracic Surgery, Orthopedic Surgery, Neurosurgery, Gynecology, Urology, Anesthesiology and Painology, Otolaryngology, Pediatrics, and Emergency Medicine. In addition, there are 44 doctors with different positions such as professor, associate professor, assistant professor, resident, and intern. Our hospital is performing organ transplant surgery as quickly and accurately as possible through organ donation in the hospital. The building has a total of 7 floors, and the 2nd to 6th floors consist of a single room as a basic ward, and the 7th floor is divided into a vip ward.
